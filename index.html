The studio

Eight years behind the same lens, still learning what it sees.

Studio Aperture started as a one-person practice photographing friends' weddings on borrowed film cameras. It's grown into a small studio that still shoots every session personally — no hand-offs, no second team. We believe a photograph earns its place by being honest before it's beautiful.

How a shoot runs

Three conversations, one afternoon.

ENQUIRY
Tell us the occasion

A short call or message about the day, the light you like, and the people involved. We confirm availability and send a simple agreement.

SESSION
We shoot, you live in the moment

On the day, our job is to disappear into the background so yours can stay present. Direction is gentle — most of our favourite frames are unposed.

DELIVERY
A gallery, not a data dump

A curated online gallery follows within 48 hours for previews, full edits within two weeks — graded, sequenced, and easy to share.
